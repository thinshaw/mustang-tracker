Gradebook

Tap any cell to set a score or mark missing / absent / makeup. Topic tags on each assignment feed the Re-teach Radar automatically. Every change saves to your account immediately.
↑ the whole app in one glance — everything else is generated from this grid

Speak Grades

Pick the assignment, then talk: "Marcus 78, Kayla absent, Josh missing, Emma 92." Review what it heard, then apply. On iPad you can also tap the text box and use the keyboard's 🎙 dictation button — that always works.

Who Owes What

Built automatically from the gradebook — no separate list to maintain. This is the tab you'd open when a kid says "wait, what am I missing?"

Re-teach Radar

Class average by topic, rolled up from every graded assignment tagged with that topic. Red flags = strong candidates to re-teach before moving on.

Class Setup

Your roster and your assignments. Add students by typing, by talking, or by pasting a CSV straight out of FACTS.

Students

Tap the ✕ on a student to remove them. Removing a student deletes their grades too.

Assignments

Class periods

Send to FACTS / RenWeb

Phase 1: download a CSV shaped for quick entry or import into FACTS — enter once here, transfer in one motion.

Phase 2 (if the school says yes): FACTS has an API, but the key has to be turned on by the school's FACTS administrator. If they'll grant one, grades could sync automatically — no double entry at all. That's a conversation with the front office, not a technical blocker.
