Gradebook

Tap any cell to set a score or mark it missing, absent, or makeup. Every change saves to your account immediately, and the other tabs update from this grid on their own.

Speak Grades

Pick the assignment, then speak: "Marcus 78, Kayla absent, Josh missing, Emma 92." Review what it heard before anything is written. On iPad you can also tap the text box and use the keyboard's dictation key — that always works.

Who Owes What

Built automatically from the gradebook — no separate list to maintain. This is the tab you'd open when a kid says "wait, what am I missing?"

Re-teach Radar

Class average by topic, rolled up from every graded assignment tagged with that topic. Red flags = strong candidates to re-teach before moving on.

Class Setup

Manage this class's roster, assignments, and periods. Changes save immediately.

Students

Last names are optional — they're only used to tell two students with the same first name apart. Removing a student also removes their grades.

Assignments

Class periods

Sample class

Adds a separate practice class — 10 fictional students, 8 assignments, a term of grades. Useful for showing someone how the app works without touching a real roster. Your existing classes aren't affected.

Send to FACTS / RenWeb

Phase 1: download a CSV shaped for quick entry or import into FACTS — enter once here, transfer in one motion.

Phase 2 (if the school says yes): FACTS has an API, but the key has to be turned on by the school's FACTS administrator. If they'll grant one, grades could sync automatically — no double entry at all. That's a conversation with the front office, not a technical blocker.
